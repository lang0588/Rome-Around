<!-- Theme -->
1. Concept: The theme will be used for a “destination showcasing website”. Your group should choose a travel destination and create a site with content and multimedia to showcase it.

2. Configuration: Theme folder is ‘cms2-project’ and theme name is ‘CMS II Project’. Add the team member’s names as the theme author.

3. Styles: All the styles go into style.css.

4. Pages: Your site must have at least 4 pages in total (home + 3). Fill the pages with content and multimedia that follows the concept. Content can be direct and short.

5. Theme screenshot: Add a theme screenshot to display on WP admin. (https://smartwp.com/wordpress-theme-screenshot/)

6. Colour palette: You must prepare a colour palette with at least 4 colours and follow it throughout the theme. Within the zip, submit a screenshot of your palette.

7. Assets: Your theme must have a minimum of 5 images including a banner image.

8. PHP files: Your theme must have at least those files: index.php, page.php, footer.php, header.php, functions.php, front-page.php

<!-- Header -->
9. Navigation: Add proper WordPress navigation menu that displays all the site pages links.

<!-- Pages -->
10. Page content: Inner pages (all but home) must display title and content from the WordPress pages.

11. Google map: Add a google map with the site destination just above the footer and after the content. It must display on all pages except the home page.

<!--  Home Page -->
12. Hero banner: Display a top hero banner.

13. Home page sections and images: Besides the hero banner, your home page must have at least 2 more sections: testimonials and another section. You must also display at least 5 images in total.

14. Video section: display a video from Vimeo.

<!-- Footer -->
15. Links on the footer: Your footer must display 3 columns of links. Use 3 arrays to store the respective 3 columns of links. Display the links by looping through the array.
